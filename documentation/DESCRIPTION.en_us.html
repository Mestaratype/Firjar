Firjar is a contemporary bilingual typeface Arabic and Latin scripts with precision and harmony. Its name, meaning compass in Arabic, reflects its design philosophy.

Firjar comes with modern curves and clean straight lines, Firjar offers a highly versatile system of styles. The type family includes a wide range of styles, spanning from condensed to extended widths and from thin to black weights, making it ideal for everything from from bold headlines, branding and UI to editorial and display use.

Firjar is also a variable font built with: - 2 axes Weight (wght) and Width (wdth).

To contribute, see github.com/MestaraType/Firjar.
